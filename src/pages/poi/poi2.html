Points of interest Da Felice

Tourists head for Trastevere, but locals know that Testaccio is the place to come for real Roman cooking. Da Felice has been serving down-home cucina romana for years.

7 km 32 mins Open/Close: 12:00/1:00 Rating: 4.3/5.0 La Pergola

High-end gourmet near the Vatican. Its Italo-centric cuisine is technically impeccable, but also light and intelligent, with few of the shock tactics of, say, Heston Blumenthal. The 10-course tasting menu , also available in a pared-back seven-course version , is a good introduction. It's open only five evenings a week, and has just 55 covers, so book ahead.

3 km 16 mins Open/Close: 12:30/1:30 Rating: 4.8/5.0 Convivium

Romans invented the ‘pinsa’ – a meal very similar to pizza but with lighter ingredients to form the dough (a mix of wheat, soy and rice flour). This dish is the main course at Convivium, a recently established pinseria in Trastevere. In this cozy little haunt, eating pinsa allows you to travel back in time and indulge in a healthy, natural, low-calorie meal with a great taste.

6 km 27 mins Open/Close: 11:00/23:30 Rating: 4.6/5.0 Birreria Trilussa

Dedicated to the local roman poet, Birreria Trilussa is placed in the heart of the district. It is a very big pub, and offers a large choice in beer and food. Also there is the possibility of taking the "pasta no stop" menu. Open every night from 18:00 to 2:00.

2 km 17 mins Open/Close: 18:00/2:00 Rating: 4.2/5.0 Colors club

Placed in Trastevere, Colors club is a very youth club in which you can drink different kind of cocktails made with artisan syrups, or play with the foosball. Open every night from 18:00 to 3:00

2 km 19 mins Open/Close: 17:00/1:30 Rating: 4.7/5.0 Goa Club

One of the best electronic clubs in town. The cutting edge programming fortified its reputation as one of the most forward-thinking venues in Italy.

7 km 32 mins Open/Close: 23:00/3:00 Rating: 4.6/5.0 Circolo degli illuminati

With 3 different clubrooms, the club offers a variety of musical genres. Room01 oriented toward Tech House, Room02 Hip Hop & Room03, a perfect place to relax with chill music.

8 km 34 mins Open/Close: 23:00/4:00 Rating: 4.4/5.0 Gus

Ready to sip cocktails and dance the night in a lounge area with a bar & dance floor. Glamorous people, elegant vibe & contemporary music await you for a night that you won’t forget.

8 km 38 mins Open/Close: 22:30/3:00 Rating: 4.6/5.0 Museo di Roma in Trastevere

Its collection focuses on popular life in Rome (18th to 20th cen.). You watch mannequins depicting scenes of Roman life. price: €6

It was the centre of political life in ancient Rome. Visit 2nd floor of the Palazzo dei Conservatori to see the famous bronze she-wolf nursing Romulus and Remus. price:€15
